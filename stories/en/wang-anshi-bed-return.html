LEVEL 05 · LOVE & MARRIAGE

Wang Anshi

—Returning the Bed

Life Principle
When dealing with a stubborn person, reasoning is often less effective than guiding them by working with their personality traits. Understanding and leveraging the other party's psychological characteristics is more effective than trying to change them by force.

⏳ The Setting

In his later years, Wang Anshi (王安石) retired to Jiangning (present-day Nanjing). Although retired, he still enjoyed official privileges. His wife, Madam Wu (吴夫人), came from the household of the Duke of Yue and had an obsessive cleanliness phobia. The couple's living habits were vastly different.

⚖ The Dilemma

Madam Wu had borrowed an official rattan bed and refused to return it after use. When county officers came to collect it, none of the attendants dared speak up. As her husband, Wang Anshi wanted to avoid a direct confrontation but also needed to resolve this awkward situation.

🎯 Motives

Wang Anshi's goal was to get Madam Wu to voluntarily return the rattan bed while avoiding a direct marital confrontation. His motive was pragmatic — solving a specific problem rather than trying to change his wife's personality.

🧩 The Framework

Wang Anshi understood that his wife's cleanliness obsession was extreme — she could not tolerate anything 'unclean.' He used this personality trait, employing his own 'unclean' behavior to trigger his wife's action of returning the bed.

🧠 The Mindset

Wang Anshi was straightforward and casual by nature, not particular about details — a stark contrast to his wife's cleanliness obsession. He did not try to persuade her with reason, but found a method of 'fighting poison with poison.'

💡 The Strategy

One day, Wang Anshi climbed onto the rattan bed barefoot and lay on it for a long while. Madam Wu saw this from a distance and immediately ordered the bed returned to the government office — she could not bear to keep a bed 'contaminated' by her husband in the house.

🔬 The Mental Model

The mental model was 'using one's own weakness to attack the other's weakness' — using something he didn't mind (being 'dirty') to trigger something she extremely minded (being 'dirty'), thereby achieving the goal. This was a precise exploitation of the other party's psychological vulnerability.

🏛 The Outcome

The rattan bed was smoothly returned, the county officer's problem was solved, and Wang Anshi avoided a direct confrontation with his wife. The entire process was clean and effortless.

💛 Why This Matters

An old couple with vastly different personalities, each using their own way to 'outwit' the other in daily life — full of the warmth of real life. Wang Anshi did not put on airs as a former prime minister, revealing a genuine and endearing side.

🔮 The Deeper Lesson

The core is 'exploiting the other party's compulsion' — when the other person has a psychological tendency they cannot control, triggering that tendency to achieve your goal. Modern marketing's 'limited-time flash sales' also exploit consumers' fear of missing out.
